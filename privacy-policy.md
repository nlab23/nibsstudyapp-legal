# Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** 1/02/2026
**Owner:** Nibs Study App
**Contact:** support@nibsstudio.com

## Data We Collect
- **App data:** Tasks, subjects, study logs, settings are stored locally on your device. We do not run our own servers for this data.
- **Purchases:** In-app purchases are processed by Apple/Google. We do not receive your full payment details.
- **Notifications:** If you enable reminders, your preference is stored locally to deliver notifications.
- **No accounts:** No sign-up or login required.

## How We Use Data
- To run the app features (tasks, subjects, timers, progress).
- To deliver optional notifications you enable.
- To process purchases via Apple/Google storefronts.

## Sharing
- We do **not** sell or share your personal data.
- Apple/Google receive only what is needed to process purchases.

## Data Storage & Retention
- Data remains on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes locally stored data.

## Permissions
- **Notifications** (optional): to send reminders you enable.
No other permissions are required for core features.

## Your Choices
- Disable notifications in the app or device settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove local data.

## Children
This app is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and have concerns, contact us at support@nibsstudio.com.

## Changes
We may update this policy and will revise the “Effective Date” above when we do.

## Contact
Questions or requests: support@nibsstudio.com
